## Phase 2: First Login (5 minutes)

- [ ] Open https://sntrendycollections.in
- [ ] Click profile icon (top right)
- [ ] Click "Login"
- [ ] Enter credentials:
  - [ ] Email: admin@sntrendycollections.in
  - [ ] Password: Qwerty@1234
- [ ] Successfully logged in ✅

---

## Phase 3: Upload First Product (10 minutes)

- [ ] Click profile icon → "Upload Product"
- [ ] Add 3-5 images of a saree
- [ ] Fill product details:
  - [ ] Product Name
  - [ ] Category (Sarees)
  - [ ] Price
  - [ ] Original Price (for discount)
  - [ ] Colors
  - [ ] Fabric
  - [ ] Occasion
  - [ ] Description
- [ ] Click "Upload Product"
- [ ] See success message ✅
- [ ] Go to homepage and verify product appears

---

## Phase 4: Test Customer Experience (10 minutes)

- [ ] Logout from admin
- [ ] Browse website as customer
- [ ] Click on your uploaded product
- [ ] Click "Order on WhatsApp" button
- [ ] Verify WhatsApp opens with correct message
- [ ] Check if your WhatsApp receives the message
