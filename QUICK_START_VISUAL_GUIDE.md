- Add 3-5 images per saree
- Show front, back, and model views

## 💡 Pro Tips for Better Sales

### 1. Good Product Names
❌ Bad: "Saree 1"
✅ Good: "Red Silk Saree with Golden Border"

### 2. Attractive Pricing
❌ Bad: Price: 2999, Original: 2999 (No discount)
✅ Good: Price: 2999, Original: 3999 (25% OFF badge)

### 3. Multiple Colors
❌ Bad: Colors: Red
✅ Good: Colors: Red, Maroon, Pink (Shows variety)

### 4. Good Description
❌ Bad: "Nice saree"
✅ Good: "Beautiful red silk saree with intricate golden border work. Perfect for weddings and special occasions. Comes with matching blouse piece."

### 5. Quality Images
✅ Front view
✅ Back view
✅ Model wearing it
✅ Close-up of design
✅ Blouse piece (if included)
